ChatGPT Bookmark

About the Extension

ChatGPT Bookmark is a Google Extension designed to help you bookmark your ChatGPT chats. This tool becomes invaluable when you need a single place to reference your work, especially if you intend to continue a particular chat session later.

Click here to download the extension.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. ChatGPT Bookmark uses your browser's local storage (similar to cookies) to store active tab URLs. Please rest assured, no personal or user-specific information is gathered from this application.
